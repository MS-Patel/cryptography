What is decentralized finance?

Decentralized finance or DeFi is an independent financial ecosystem that allows its users to invest, trade, buy and sell cryptocurrencies eliminating the middlemen from transactions.

Why choose a company that follows a decentralized structure?

A decentralized company has a transparent and efficient financial structure. It is secured by blockchain technology that ensures encrypted transactions. This technology helps in reducing scams, frauds, corruption etc.
